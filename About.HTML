About NJIT Spot On

Welcome to NJIT Spot On, your dedicated parking solution at New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT). Our platform is designed to simplify and streamline your parking experience on campus.

Our Mission

At NJIT Spot On, our mission is to provide convenient and efficient parking services to ensure you can park on time, every time. We understand the challenges of parking on a busy campus, and our goal is to make the process as seamless as possible.

Services We Offer

Explore our website to discover the range of services we offer:

  • Booking Parking Spaces: Reserve your parking space in advance to guarantee availability.
  • Payment Solutions: Make secure online payments for your parking transactions.
